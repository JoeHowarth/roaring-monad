Publication Flow, Leases, Failover, and Crash Recovery

This is a visual model of the current publication-state design: one authoritative publication record, one active writer, hot standby RW nodes, immutable artifact writes, and reader visibility only after publication CAS advances the finalized head.

Reader + Writer can act as standby, renew the lease, or take over after expiry
Healthy Primary Rule fresh leases block opportunistic takeover by new RW nodes

The controls below switch between the main operational paths: steady publication, standby startup, lease-expiry takeover, and crash recovery before republish.
